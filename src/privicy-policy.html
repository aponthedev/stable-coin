Privacy Policy

Your trust is central to everything we build. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage information when you use our stablecoin payment platform, card features, website, and related digital services.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details, identity verification information, contact details, device data, transaction activity, wallet or card usage data, and support communications. We only collect information that helps us operate securely, meet compliance obligations, improve the product, and deliver a reliable financial experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to create and manage your account, process payments, support card controls, monitor transactions, prevent fraud, personalize product experiences, respond to support requests, and maintain platform performance. We also use limited data for analytics, risk management, legal reporting, and service improvement.

Security and Fraud Protection

We apply modern security practices designed for financial technology environments, including encryption, access controls, monitoring, secure infrastructure, and fraud detection workflows. While no system can guarantee absolute protection, we work continuously to reduce risk and protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

Sharing With Trusted Partners

We may share information with carefully selected service providers, payment processors, banking or card program partners, compliance vendors, infrastructure providers, analytics tools, and customer support systems. We do not sell your personal information, and we require partners to handle data responsibly and only for authorized purposes.

Compliance and Legal Requirements

Because we operate in a financial services environment, we may process data to support identity checks, sanctions screening, anti-money laundering controls, dispute handling, regulatory requests, audits, and other legal obligations. These controls help protect users, partners, and the integrity of the platform.

Your Choices and Data Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of eligible personal information, subject to applicable law and financial recordkeeping requirements. You can also manage certain communication preferences from your account settings or by contacting our support team.

Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed to provide services, protect the platform, resolve disputes, meet legal and compliance obligations, and maintain accurate business records. When data is no longer required, we delete, anonymize, or securely archive it according to our internal retention practices.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact our support team at support@stablecoin.com. We review privacy requests carefully and aim to respond with clarity, transparency, and respect for your rights.
